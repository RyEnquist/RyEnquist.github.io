Alarm.com

Project Brief

Alarm.com is an incredibly innovative company that operates in the home security industry. Through ground breaking technology and cloud services, they offer homeowners a unique home wireless security experience that helps keep families safe. Alarm.com is an active participant in security-based trade show events across the country. As a result, I had the luxury of working with this great company on some of their larger shows. ISC West (International Security Conference) is the largest security conference in the world. They needed to make a splash. The goal was simple: Build a beautiful booth under budget, that would draw eyes from everyone.

As a lead Project Manager, I helped manage a cross-functional team in the planning, design, production, labor, and logistics of this booth. There’s nothing quite like a trade show project deadline, if you miss the in-hand date you miss the show… Needless to say we couldn’t afford any bottlenecks. Ultimately through effective planning, and constant communication with stakeholders across the board this project went off without a hitch!

Conclusion

With a tight budget, and limited hours I helped produce a trade show booth that was a major participant in one of the largest shows in the country. Alarm.com was a huge hit and was one of the finalist in design awards. As a result of everything, Alarm.com was able to generate significant leads that ultimately lead to sales.
