A World of Tile

Project Brief

A World of Tile is a tile retailer based out of Denver, CO. They have 16 locations, and a thriving ecommerce site. With no on-site “Marketer”, they sought the assistance of our Marketing Agency. I served as the primary, full service Marketing Manager for the organization.

My primary objective was to increase both website traffic and sales on a quarterly basis. I organized and executed various projects of all shapes, sizes, and budgets. I handled company wide marketing strategy, email campaigns, display advertising, SEO, website maintenance, social media, storefront marketing collateral (posters, brochures, product display, etc.), sales, contests, and opening of new retail store locations.


Conclusion

With limited hours to allocate to Marketing efforts, I made efficient use of time and resources to help promote web traffic and increase overall sales. Through sound marketing and advertising strategy, A World of Tile was able to realize an increase in sales on annual basis and capture more market share from their competitors.
