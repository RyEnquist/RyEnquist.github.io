Project Brief

Devil Mountain is a wholesale nursery based out of San Ramon, CA. In desperate need of a brand refresh, they sought the assistance of a Marketing Agency. I was lucky enough to take the lead on this project. I served as the primary Project Manager acting as the liaison between the client and our agency support staff.

During the project, I helped orchestrate a complete brand overhaul, which led to the logo and tagline you see today: DEVIL MOUNTAIN: WHATEVER THE NEED. WE DELIVER.

I helped aid in the redesign of their website, which focused on an intuitive and efficient plant catalog for landscapers and the like to utilize when placing orders. In addition, I helped create a series of engaging web videos for commercial/website use, designed/produced new and exciting marketing collateral, organized/executed both an email and social media campaign, all the while serving as their primary marketing strategist.
